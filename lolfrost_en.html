Dark Code - Level 4

You sit in front of the rusty terminal. Green lines blink on the screen as if they are breathing. Next to the keyboard, on a pile of dust and forgotten cables, you find an old journal with a cracked cover. You open it. One page is marked with a red tab. You begin reading:

There were two sisters who liked to be the first at everything, they always gave their best,
sometimes followed by his lumberjack friend.

Since they were little, they played Power Rangers and made transformations.
One was very kind and friendly, while the other was quite the opposite.

They never saw color television because their parents never bought one and the one they had was very old,
and it made an incredibly loud noise.
Finally, they realized how many people they left behind.

You close the journal. Everything seems personal. Too concrete to be a coincidence. Perhaps these words, this puzzle, are the last barrier between you... and the hacker’s trail. You are closer. You just need to see what doesn’t fit.

"A broken story leaves clues for those who know how to listen."
